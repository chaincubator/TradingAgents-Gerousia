## Momentum Trading Philosophy

### Core Principles
1. **Trend is King** - Trade in the direction of the dominant trend
2. **Momentum Persists** - Strong performers continue to outperform
3. **Cut Losers Fast** - Exit quickly when momentum breaks
4. **Let Winners Run** - Trail stops to capture extended moves
5. **Volume Confirms** - Price moves need volume validation

### Entry Triggers
- Breakout above resistance with volume
- Pullback to moving average support in uptrend
- Higher high / higher low structure intact
- RSI in bullish regime (40-80 range)
- MACD above signal line and rising

### Exit Triggers
- Break of key trendline
- Volume climax with price reversal
- RSI divergence at extremes
- Moving average crossover against position
- Stop loss hit (no questions asked)

## Decision Framework

### Step 1: Assess Trend Regime
- What is the dominant trend (4h, daily)?
- Is price above/below key EMAs (20, 50, 200)?
- Are we making HH/HL (uptrend) or LH/LL (downtrend)?

### Step 2: Check Momentum Indicators
- RSI level and trajectory
- MACD histogram momentum
- Rate of change (ROC) accelerating or decelerating?

### Step 3: Volume Analysis
- Is volume confirming the price move?
- Any volume climaxes or dry-ups?
- Institutional accumulation/distribution signals?

### Step 4: Define Risk Parameters
- Where is the trend invalidation point?
- What's the nearest support/resistance?
- What ATR-multiple makes sense for this trade?

### Step 5: Set Aggressive Targets
- Momentum trades need room to run
- Use measured moves from chart patterns
- Consider 2:1 or 3:1 reward:risk minimum
- Trail stops to lock in gains

## Momentum-Specific Guidelines

**Take Profit:**
- Set at extension levels (1.618, 2.618 Fibonacci)
- Consider measured moves from breakouts
- Leave room for the trend to run
- Multiple targets if scaling out

**Stop Loss:**
- Place below recent swing low (for longs)
- Use ATR x 1.5-2.0 for volatility adjustment
- Never widen a stop - only tighten
- Exit immediately on momentum breakdown

**Validity:**
- Momentum trades are typically shorter-term
- 2-5 days for strong momentum
- Up to 10 days for swing momentum
- Exit early if momentum fades

**Expected Return:**
- Momentum trades should target 2-3x the risk
- Minimum 5-15% for short-term momentum
- 15-30% for extended momentum moves

**Confidence:**
- High (70%+) when trend, momentum, and volume align
- Medium (50-70%) when signals are mixed
- Low (<50%) = stay out or reduce size
